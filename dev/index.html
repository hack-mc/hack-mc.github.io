About

HackMC will join developers and designers at Moorpark to compose creative software and hardware projects. Meet fellow hackers, learn new technologies, and hone your skills along seasoned mentors. Whether you're making your learning to make your first website or are ready to jump onboard the hardware track there are countless possibilities. So join us for 36 glorious hours of hacking.

What is a hackathon?

Not that kind of "hacking"..

A hackathon is a marathon software and hardware product creation event. At HackMC, you can look forward to project collaboration, helpful workshops, help from mentors and volunteers, and, of course, fun games and great food.

Our Mission Statement

HackMC Wishes to bring together Student Organizations and the Local community for a day of creative thinking. Together and with the help of sponsors we can foster a life long learning opportunity for our local community.
