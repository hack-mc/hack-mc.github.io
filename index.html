What is a hackathon?

Not that kind of "hacking"..

A hackathon is a marathon software and hardware product creation event. At HackMC, you can look forward to project collaboration, helpful workshops, help from mentors and volunteers, and of course fun games and great food.

Our Mission Statement

HackMC Wishes to bring together Student Organizations and the Local community for a day of creative thinking. Together and with the help of sponsors we can foster a life long learning opportunity for our local community.

FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an overnight event where students get together in teams to create an awesome new technology. Prizes are awarded by judges after the building process is over, and there are also workshops during the event to help beginners learn more.

Do I need previous experience?

Absolutely not! Hackathons are for beginners as well as seasoned developers. If you don't have experience with programming, we'll have plenty of workshops where you'll be able to pick up the basics.

How do I get there?

HackMC will be located at Moorpark College Moorpark, CA. The event will take place in the school's Cafeteria. If you'd like a map, then click here. We'll have staff outside the building that'll direct you to the main event, as well as give you a map of the school in case you get lost.

 

Who is allowed to attend?

college students, highschoolers, and the general public is allowed to attend. If you feel you're too old or young to attend, but have experience, you can contact us. All we ask is that attendees be respectful of one another throughout the event.

What should I bring?

Be sure to bring a laptop and a cellphone with chargers, as these 2 devices are what you'll mainly be using during the event. They're pretty important if you'll want to hack. Sleeping bags and power strips will be convenient as well.

How much will it cost?

It's free, of course! Hackathons are supposed to be a learning experience, and we don't think there should be a price on education.
